What’s the opportunity?

Local business runs on repeat customers. But feedback is mostly invisible.

The real pain

Owners know sales are slow, but they don’t know whether the issue is food, service, pricing, hygiene, ambience, delivery experience or Google reviews.

The silent gap

Most unhappy customers never complain. They leave quietly, tell someone else, or choose another place next time.

Our wedge

Start with a free diagnosis. Then convert serious owners into a ₹99 improvement report and a QR-based live feedback system.

Why now

India’s local businesses already understand QR, WhatsApp and Google reviews. We add the missing layer: customer truth converted into action.

Here’s how it works

A simple funnel that starts free, proves value quickly, and becomes recurring revenue once feedback starts flowing.
